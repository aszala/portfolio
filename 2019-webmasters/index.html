Welcome to Panther Creek High School, the home of the Catamounts! Since its inception in 2006, the Panther Creek High School community has been committed to engaging students in opportunities and experiences that will prepare them for future challenges, and fostered a sense of community through curricular and extra-curricular programs. Here at Panther Creek, we appreciate the ordinary, pursue the extraordinary, and celebrate progress and achievement. Go Catamounts!
