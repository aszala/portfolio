About

I’ve been a passionate engineer for most of my life. If there’s one thing that I want to do more than anything else, it’s to create new technologies that can make the world better. For me, creation is one of the best things in life.

The goal of many of my projects so far is simply to learn more and be innovative. I find that I learn the most by experimenting and seeing my work in action. While learning from classes and reading is very important, I feel that I have learned the most through my experimentation.

I grew in Cary, NC, near an area with large growth in the technology industry. I spend a lot of my spare time developing new software, making new video games, and 3D modeling. Whether I am creating for fun or work, I find satisfaction in anything I make.
