Mr. Curran - Game Art Design and Digital Design and Animation Teacher and TSA Advisor

"Make Good Decisions" - Brent Curran

Mr. Curran is in his second year of education and advising at Panther Creek High School. He spent seven years in management before transitioning to education. Mr. Curran's interest in education comes from his work with middle schoolers and Lego robotics in the Lenoir County Public School System. Mr. Curran was the founder of SKATEch for the City of Raleigh Parks and Recreation, a summer camp that blends the art of videography and photography to skateboarding.

Dr. Busby - Technology, Engineering, and Design Teacher and TSA Advisor

"I recognized a future in education as making a difference in students' lives. I enjoy helping students develop skills that will follow them throughout their careers." - Joe Busby

Dr. Joe R. Busby is currently in his 38th year in education. His teaching experience includes middle and high school, undergraduate and graduate education. He also spent 5 years as a teacher supervisor with the Department of Education in Mississippi and State Advisor for TSA in 2 other states. Dr. Busby has served in numerous leadership roles at the local, state, national, and international levels, and has received the honors of Life Member in MS TSA, Laurate Citation in Epsilon Pi Taw, and Distinguished Technology Educator in the International Technology Engineering Educators Association.

Dana Kaushik - Project Management, Marketing, Excel, and Principle of Business and Finance

"Enjoy the little things in life for one day you'll look back and realize they were big things" - Kurt Vonnegut

My dream as a kid was to become a veterinarian. I applied to Purdue University and was accepted in the pre-vet program. During the summer between my freshman and sophomore year I had an internship at Disney World. This experience forever changed my life and I was I immediately went back to school and changed my major to Elementary Education. After college, I accepted a position as a 2nd grade teacher. However, I really had my heart set on teaching 4th grade. The following year a position opened up and I continued to teach 4th grade for the next 10 years. Wanting to challenge myself, I went back to grad school and received an MBA from Florida Tech.

Dan Nolan - Technology, Engineering, and Design Teacher

Teaching experience in the inner city of Baltimore. Taught life skills, coping skills and specialized in behavior modification in Child Psychiatry at University of Maryland Medical Center. Started a Non Profit and consult small businesses on Social Media Strategy. High School lacrosse coach and private lacrosse coach.

Joesph Bizzell - Microsoft Word and PowerPoint and Principle of Business and Fianance Teacher

"I want to share the opportunities that I didn't have while I was in high school with the students of today...students can use them in their lives and there are so many ways to share that information." - Joesph Bizzell

Mr. Joseph Bizzell is a CTE teacher at Panther Creek High School. He teaches Microsoft Word and PowerPoint, as well as Principles of Business and Finance. He has an MBA in education, and also teaches as an Adjunct Professor at Saint Augustine's College. He is dedicated to teaching students skills inside the fields of business and technology, and believes that TSA gives students the opportunity to work with technology, and to make a difference in the world.
