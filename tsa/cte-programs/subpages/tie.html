Trade and Industrial Education
Trade and Industrial Education provides students the opportunity to advance in a wide range of trade and industrial occupations. They are prepared for initial employment, further education at the community college or university level, and/or business ownership.

Digital Media
Digital Media is the modern format for conveying any message, entertainment or information. This class provides students a strong introduction to developing their own digital media products complete with audio, visuals, animations, transitions, effects and all fundamentals of modern digital media production. The class is mainly structured on concepts and lessons, however offers plenty of projects to allow students to implement what they have learned and express their new concepts in forms they choose.
Advanced Digital Media Honors
The Advanced Digital Media course offered at Panther Creek takes the concepts learned in the first class and focuses on applying them in a more professional design aspect. The projects have an emphasis on social media and marketing or business contexts. This level II course is perfect for preparing students for careers such as advertising and marketing, business presentations, or social media specialist.
