About Isaiah
Isaiah Salek


Isaiah Salek is a musical artist from Cary, North Carolina. He found his passion for making music through Panther Creek TSAâ€™s Music Production event, and eventually combined his instrumental talent with his vocal abilities to write songs that speak to the younger generation. Isaiah wants to spread positivity in the world, and to uplift anyone that feels down. He has always wanted to use his voice to create positive change in society, and now music gives him a platform to do so. Even with his recent success, Isaiah still remains dedicated to his school, as well as his local town of Cary, where he performs at local events and gives away free concert tickets and music to fans.
